## PaillacoDiff

PaillacoDiff is Wolfram Mathematica package that allow to do computations of (pseudo-)Riemannian geometry and differential forms for a given chart.

For instance, if you have coordinates $$x^\mu = (t,r,x,y)$$, then you have a basis of the 1-form space $$\dd x^\mu$$, and you can construct higher-degree forms using the wedge product.
