Talk about the paper Quadratic gravity and conformally coupled scalar field (https://arxiv.org/abs/2001.01478). In the context of the school of gravitation at CECs Valdivia. I presented together with Nicolas Caceres (co-author of the paper), the theory and mechanics under which we manage to construct black holes solutions in the quadratic theory of gravity coupled coformally to a scalar field.
