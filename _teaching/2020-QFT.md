The course was lectured by Prof. Julio Oliva. It was intended as an introductory course to Quantum Field Theory, and my role was to cover prerequisite material needed to follow the main content. This involved reviewing selected topics from classical mechanics, classical field theory and quantum mechanics. Since the course took place during the pandemic, I recorded and edited the lectures to make them easier and more enjoyable for students to follow. Links to the lectures are provided below.
