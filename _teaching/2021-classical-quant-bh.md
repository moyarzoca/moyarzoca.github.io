The Summer School on Classical and Quantum Black Holes featured lectures delivered by Prof. Andrés Anabalón and Prof. Gastón Giribet. It was held online, and I was a co-organizer. To facilitate access to the material, I prepared detailed lecture notes in LaTeX and made them publicly available on the school’s webpage.

You can find the notes on classical black holes by Prof. Anabalón [here](/files/BH_AA_full.pdf), and the notes on quantum black holes by Prof. Giribet [here](/files/BH_GG_full.pdf).
